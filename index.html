Pricing

At the conference this year, we're offering something a little new. 4 tiers of experience, tailored to the particular needs of our audience.

  • The Platinum Experience is designed for the most engaged members of our industry, looking to immerse themselves in the near future, and maximize their investment of their very limited time.
  • The Gold Experience is designed for Senior Creative, Strategic and decision makers
  • The Silver Experience is tailored for passionate Industry professionals looking to connect and explore, and make their (or their team’s) experience even more valuable.
  • The Classic Experience is what you've always known, but even better. Ideal for freelancers, educators and others looking to make their budget work as hard as possible.
